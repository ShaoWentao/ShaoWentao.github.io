HSKC2026 Seoul recap: hospitality lighting conversations, regional project references and the next stop in Malaysia.
A rewarding day at the inaugural HSKC2026 in Seoul
Hospitality lighting conversations in South Korea
Dear partners and friends,
CDN Lighting was pleased to take part in the inaugural Hospitality South Korea Conference 2026 in Seoul. The event brought together hospitality professionals from South Korea and the wider region, creating a valuable platform for knowledge sharing, project discussion and new industry connections.
What stood out most was the quality of the conversations. From conference sessions to booth discussions, attendees came with clear project interests and a strong willingness to exchange ideas on how lighting can shape more engaging and memorable hospitality experiences.
Thank you for visiting the CDN booth
We sincerely thank the organizers, partners and everyone who visited the CDN Lighting booth. The exchanges with professionals across hospitality operations, ownership, investment, design and project delivery gave us fresh perspective on the evolving needs of hotel and commercial spaces in the region.

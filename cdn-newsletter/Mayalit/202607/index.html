About Mayalit and CDN Lighting
Mayalit is a specialized museum lighting brand under CDN Lighting, focusing on professional lighting solutions for museums, galleries, showcases, cultural exhibitions and public display spaces.
This online training is part of CDN Lighting's partner development support. The goal is to provide clear product knowledge, application logic and presentation materials so partners can identify suitable project opportunities and communicate museum lighting value more effectively.

What the session will cover
The training will focus on the information partners need when opening museum and exhibition lighting conversations:
01 Mayalit positioning and the key requirements of museum lighting projects.
02 Product systems for display cases, spot lighting, wall washing, framing, gobo and track applications.
03 How to explain visual comfort, high color rendering, precise beam control, dimming and modular accessories.
04 Selected project references and practical communication points for partner presentations.
Reference case: National Museum of China
The National Museum of China reference shows how controlled light can support cultural display, object visibility and spatial atmosphere at the same time. In museum environments, lighting is not only about brightness. It is also about color fidelity, glare control, beam accuracy and the long-term viewing experience.
During the session, we will use selected references like this to discuss how Mayalit solutions can be positioned for cultural projects and how partners can translate technical advantages into clear project value.
A product system built for exhibition lighting
Mayalit products are developed around the needs of museum and exhibition spaces, including high color rendering, controlled beam options, modular optical accessories, flexible adjustment, dimming support and clean integration with professional track systems.
The training will introduce product families such as Pax, Ben, Ajaw, Xul mini, Zac, Imix, Cimi, Sek and Mol, with a focus on how each system supports different museum lighting tasks.

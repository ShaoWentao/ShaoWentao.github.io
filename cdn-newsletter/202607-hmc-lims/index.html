Two inspiring stops across the region
Lighting Innovation + Modern Buildings Summit 2026 and HMC2026
Dear partners and friends,
CDN Lighting recently joined two valuable industry gatherings across the region: the Lighting Innovation + Modern Buildings Summit 2026 in Muscat and HMC2026 in Kuala Lumpur. Both events brought together consultants, contractors, developers, owners, designers and industry partners for practical conversations around the future of lighting and built environments.
From sustainable urban development to hospitality project needs, the exchanges gave us fresh perspectives on how intelligent lighting solutions can support more efficient, comfortable and future-ready spaces.

Great conversations in Muscat
At the Lighting Innovation + Modern Buildings Summit 2026, CDN Lighting was pleased to exchange ideas with professionals from Oman and the wider Middle East. Discussions covered project application, smart building development, sustainability and the role of lighting in creating better user experiences.
We sincerely thank the organizers and everyone who visited us during the summit. As a professional lighting solution provider, CDN Lighting looks forward to continuing these conversations and supporting the next generation of lighting projects across the region.
Another inspiring stop: HMC2026
The Hospitality Malaysia Conference 2026 in Kuala Lumpur created another valuable opportunity to meet hospitality professionals from across the region. Together with our local partners, CDN Lighting shared selected project references and practical lighting solutions for hospitality, commercial and lifestyle spaces.
Thank you to everyone who visited the CDN booth and shared your insights with us. We appreciate every conversation and look forward to turning new connections into future collaboration.

1) 2025 Overseas Business Update
In 2025, our overseas business delivered solid growth, driven by strong execution and close collaboration across our teams and partners worldwide. This achievement marks another step forward in expanding CDN Lighting’s global footprint.
To support long-term overseas development, CDN Lighting maintains localized operations across key international markets. Currently, we operate four overseas subsidiaries in Indonesia, Vietnam, the United Arab Emirates, and Saudi Arabia, along with a regional office in Singapore. This international presence enables closer collaboration with partners and more responsive local support.

• Enhanced global brand presence through strategic marketing and partner engagement

3) Domestic Channel Price Adjustment Notice
A price adjustment will be implemented in domestic sales channels starting from March 1, 2026, mainly in response to sustained increases in key raw material costs such as copper and aluminum.
Note for overseas partners
Overseas pricing will remain unchanged at this stage. Following the completion of the domestic adjustment, overseas pricing will be reviewed accordingly based on the updated global price framework, with an expected adjustment in Q2 2026.
